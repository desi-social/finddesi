# Find Desi
[**Find Desi**](https://desicube.com/india) is a community-driven platform that helps Indians and South Asians connect with desi people near them across the USA, Canada, Australia, Europe, and around the world. Whether you are searching for local friends, cultural groups, parenting discussions, community events, career networking, food recommendations, or support in a new city, [**Find Desi**](https://desicube.com/india) brings people together through meaningful conversations and shared experiences. It is a place where you can stay connected to your roots, exchange ideas, celebrate traditions, and build friendships with people who understand your culture and lifestyle. No matter where you live, Find Desi helps you discover a welcoming desi community nearby and across the globe.
